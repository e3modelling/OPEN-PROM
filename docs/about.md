OPEN-PROM is developed by [E3Modelling](https://e3modelling.com); it is the open-source version of the PROMETHEUS
model. For a description of the model, see the {doc}`Overview <overview>` and the model
{doc}`Introduction </model/01_introduction>`.

## Acknowledgements and funding

OPEN-PROM was originally developed within the European Union's Horizon programme in the **DIAMOND** project (Grant
Agreement No. 101081179) and has been further advanced in **TRANSIENCE** (Grant Agreement No. 101137606), which
enhanced its industrial representation and its treatment of circular-economy policies and impacts.

Funded by the European Union. Views and opinions expressed are however those of the author(s) only and do not
necessarily reflect those of the European Union or the granting authority. Neither the European Union nor the
granting authority can be held responsible for them.

## How to cite

When citing OPEN-PROM, please refer to the citation metadata provided in the `HOWTOCITE.cff` file in the
repository (OPEN-PROM, E3Modelling, version 2.2.0).
