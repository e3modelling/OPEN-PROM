## Regionalization in OPEN-PROM

This tutorial is dedicated to the classification of World countries into the 39 OPEN-PROM regions.

Custom native region codes are highlighted, while all other countries are identified using their official three-letter ISO codes.

A machine-readable version of this mapping is available here: https://raw.githubusercontent.com/e3modelling/mrprom/main/inst/extdata/regional/regionmappingOPDEV5.csv
